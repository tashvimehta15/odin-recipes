Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a classic North Indian flatbread stuffed with spicy mashed potatoes and cooked on a tawa with ghee or butter. Served with curd and pickle, it's straight-up soul food.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water (for kneading)
  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or butter (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Knead the wheat flour with water to make a soft dough. Let it rest for 15‚Äì20 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes with chili, ginger, coriander, garam masala, amchur, and salt.
  3. Take a small ball of dough, roll it out slightly, place some potato filling in the center, and seal it up.
  4. Gently roll it out into a flat paratha. Use flour to prevent sticking.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa. Add ghee/butter and flip till both sides are golden brown.
