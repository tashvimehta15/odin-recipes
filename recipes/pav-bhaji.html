Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a spicy blend of vegetables cooked in butter and served with soft bread rolls (pav). It's the ultimate comfort food from the streets of Mumbai.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter (plus more for toasting pav)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lemon wedges & onions (on the side)
  • 4 pav buns

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions until translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes. Cook until mushy.
  3. Add bell pepper, peas, mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  4. Sprinkle pav bhaji masala, chili powder, salt. Add water and mash everything together.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Toast pav buns with butter on a griddle.
  7. Garnish bhaji with coriander. Serve hot with pav, lemon wedges & chopped onions.
